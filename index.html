What is Makhana?

Makhana (Euryale ferox) is a flowering plant from the water lily family, cultivated primarily in the marshy wetlands of Bihar, India. Also known as foxnut or popped lotus seed, Makhana is a highly nutritious superfood rich in protein, magnesium, potassium, iron, and more.

  • Low fat, high carbs
  • Good source of calcium and fibre
  • Helps with digestion and aging
  • Great for diabetics
  • Used in rituals, desserts, curries, and healthy snacks
